This research has compared the performance of a transformer-based model, BERT, in predicting the customer review scores to the traditional methods of Logistic Regression (LR) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) based on the Hilton Hotels UK dataset. The study identifies that conventional machine learning approaches often fail to grasp the nuance and emotional complexity of customer reviews. According to the analysis, BERT outperforms LR and LSTM in all metrics of accuracy, precision, f-1 score, mean absolute error and is particularly effective in unravelling the complex emotions and subtle patterns in customer feedback. The research also seeks to assist management decision making by developing a BERT-based algorithm. The proposed machine learning tool will help to analyze negative sentiment reviews and guide the managers to take more informed decisions and increase customer satisfaction ratings abiding the GDPR principles.
